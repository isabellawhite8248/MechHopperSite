About

The Mechanical Hopper is a band based in providence Rhode Island which performs covers and original songs live. The band is rock and roll with a hint of indie storytelling. The band also posts covers on youtube and for each cover there is an original animation/artwork. This art is based off of an original pencil sketch and merch is available for purchase on the mechanical hopper redbubble store (link available at the bottom of the website).

Welcome to the gang, enjoy :)
